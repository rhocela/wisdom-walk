# Noah's Ark Dad Jokes

Why did Noah have to discipline the chickens on the ark?

Because they were using *fowl* language! 

<!--truncate-->

---

**Q:** What kind of man was Boaz before he married Ruth?  
**A:** Ruthless!

---

**Q:** Why didn't they play cards on Noah's ark?  
**A:** Because Noah was standing on the deck!

---

**Q:** What do you call a sleepwalking nun?  
**A:** A roamin' Catholic!

---

**Q:** Why didn't Noah go fishing?  
**A:** He only had two worms!

---

**Q:** What's the best way to study the Bible?  
**A:** You Luke into it!

---

**Q:** Why did the unemployed man get excited while reading his Bible?  
**A:** He thought he saw a Job!

---

**Q:** What kind of car would Jesus drive?  
**A:** A Christ-ler!
