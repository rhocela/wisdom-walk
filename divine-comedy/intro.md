# Divine Comedy

<div className="subtitle">Joy, laughter, and the lighter side of faith</div>

**Welcome to Divine Comedy!**

Sometimes the best theology comes with a smile! This section celebrates the joy, humor, and lighter moments that faith brings to life.

## What You'll Find

- **Biblical Humor**: Discovering God's wit and irony throughout Scripture
- **Sacred Smiles**: Clean, faith-based humor that brings joy without irreverence
- **Joyful Reflections**: Finding the lighter side of Christian life and faith
- **Holy Hilarity**: Moments when faith meets fun in delightful ways

## Our Heart

This space is created with:
- **Deep Reverence**: Never mocking God, but celebrating the joy He brings
- **Clean Fun**: Humor that builds up rather than tears down
- **Biblical Foundation**: Even our laughter connects to Scripture's truth
- **Community Spirit**: Sharing the joy that comes from knowing God

## Why Laughter Matters

God created joy, laughter, and humor as gifts to His people:
- Laughter is medicine for the soul
- Joy is a fruit of the Spirit
- God Himself rejoices over His people
- The gospel is ultimately good news - cause for celebration!

Come and smile with us as we explore the delightful, unexpected, and joyful side of walking with God.
