# Welcome to Parallels

As I've been reading through Scripture, I keep seeing these amazing patterns. Stories that echo each other. Events that mirror one another. Characters whose lives seem to follow the same divine script.

These aren't coincidences.

<!--truncate-->

## What Are Biblical Parallels?

Biblical parallels are recurring patterns, themes, and structures that appear throughout Scripture. They're like threads woven through the entire Bible, connecting Genesis to Revelation.

Some examples:
- Adam and Jesus (the first and last Adam)
- The three types of temptation in Eden and the wilderness
- Joseph's story foreshadowing Christ's journey
- The Red Sea crossing and baptism
- The tabernacle and Christ's body

## Why Do They Matter?

These patterns show us:
- God's consistent character and methods
- How the Old Testament points to Christ
- The unity and divine authorship of Scripture
- Deeper layers of meaning in familiar passages
