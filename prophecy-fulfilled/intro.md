# Prophecy Fulfilled

<div className="subtitle">God's sovereignty demonstrated through time</div>

**Welcome to Prophecy Fulfilled!**

This section explores the remarkable fulfillment of biblical prophecies throughout history, demonstrating God's sovereignty, faithfulness, and perfect timing across the ages.

## What We'll Explore

- **Messianic Prophecies**: Old Testament predictions fulfilled in Jesus Christ
- **Historical Prophecies**: Predictions about nations, rulers, and events that came to pass
- **Prophetic Timelines**: Understanding God's timing in prophetic fulfillment
- **Archaeological Evidence**: Historical and archaeological confirmations of prophetic fulfillment
- **Patterns of Fulfillment**: How God's prophetic promises demonstrate His character

## Our Foundation

Every study here rests on:
- **Scripture's Authority**: The Bible as the ultimate source of prophetic truth
- **Historical Accuracy**: Careful examination of historical records and evidence
- **God's Faithfulness**: Seeing how fulfilled prophecy reveals God's unchanging character
- **Future Hope**: Understanding how past fulfillments give confidence for future promises

## Why This Matters

Fulfilled prophecy is one of the strongest evidences for:
- The divine inspiration of Scripture
- God's active involvement in human history
- The reliability of future promises
- The supremacy of Christ as the promised Messiah
