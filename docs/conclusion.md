What began in Genesis with God creating paradise and walking with humanity concludes in Revelation with paradise restored and God dwelling with His people forever. Every book you've read contributes to this magnificent narrative:

- **The Problem:** Sin separates humanity from God
- **The Promise:** God will provide a way of redemption  
- **The Plan:** Through covenants, law, prophets, and ultimately Christ
- **The Provision:** Jesus Christ - the Word made flesh
- **The Purpose:** Restoration of all things in the new creation
